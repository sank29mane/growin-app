## 2024-05-24 - Restoring Accessibility to `.plain` Styled Buttons
**Learning:** In SwiftUI, applying `.buttonStyle(.plain)` to a `Button` completely strips it of its standard VoiceOver traits and announcements. This causes icon-only buttons or visually complex custom buttons to become opaque or confusing to screen reader users, often reading out generic unhelpful text or nothing at all.
**Action:** Whenever using `.buttonStyle(.plain)` on a button, always manually re-apply `.accessibilityLabel("...")` (if it's an icon or needs a specific name), `.accessibilityHint("...")` (for extra context), and critically, `.accessibilityAddTraits([.isSelected])` if the button acts as a selectable toggle or tab.
